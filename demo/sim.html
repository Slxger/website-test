You are the Captain

Take command of one the world’s most popular jet airliners - the Airbus A320!
You will sit in the Captain’s seat and fly from take-off to landing under the guidance of one of our commercial pilots.
With hundreds of airports available, choose where you want to fly​.

Our simulator is an accurate replica of a real Airbus A320 cockpit fitted with geniune Airbus sidesticks. Systems operation and flight dynamics are reproduced using original Airbus systems. This paired with our 220 degree wrap around HD visual system provide for an unrivalled and truly unique experience.

From flying through some of the worlds most scenic landscapes to landing challenging arrivals, we can guarantee you'll enjoy your experience. Virtually any scenario is possible. Just let us know what you would like to do and we will do our best to recreate it.
